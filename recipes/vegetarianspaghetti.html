Vegetarian Spaghetti

Description

This easy to make spaghetti sauce is a great healthy alternative to the typical meat sauce recipe. Homemade sauce taste much better than store bought jarred sauce and will impress anyone you cook this for.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 lb. Pasta of choice
  • 3 Zucchini chopped
  • 6 oz sliced White Mushrooms
  • 1 Yellow Onion diced
  • 4 Garlic Cloves minced
  • 1 (28 oz.) can Crushed Tomatoes
  • 1 (15 oz.) can Tomato Sauce
  • 1 (6 oz.) can Tomato Paste
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmasean
  • 1 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1 tbsp dried Basil Leaves
  • 2 tsp dried Oregano Leaves
  • 1 tbsp dried Rosamary Leaves
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Add 2 tbsp olive oil to a large, high-sided skillet and place over medium-high heat for 2 minutes. Add the zucchini, mushrooms, and onion. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Add the garlic. Cook for one minute, stirring often.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, water, sugar, basil, oregano, rosemary, 1 tbsp olive oil, salt and pepper. Stir to combine.
  4. Reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for one hour and a half, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add parmesean cheese, cover and simmer for another 30 minutes.
  6. While the sauce is simmering for the last 30 minutes, bring a large pot of water to a boil with a tbsp of olive oil and salt to taste.
  7. Once water is boiling add pasta, cook until al dente, about 10 minutes depending on type of pasta used.
  8. Serve sauce over pasta, top with parmasean if desired.
